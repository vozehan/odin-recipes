Watermelon Salad Recipe

Description

This is a watermelon salad recipe from Love and Lemons, a well-known recipe site created by Jeanine Donofrio. The delicious combo is the perfect blend of sweet and savory for a summer side dish, or refreshing snack.

Ingredients

For the dressing...

  • 2 tablespoons EVOO
  • 3 tablespoons Lime Juice
  • 1/2 Garlic Clove, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon Sea Salt

For the salad...

  • 5 cups cubed Watermelon
  • 1 heaping cup Diced English Cucumber
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced Red Onions
  • 1/3 cup crumbled Feta Cheese
  • 1 Avocado, cubed
  • 1/3 cup torn Mint or Basil Leaves
