Broccoli Recipe

Description

This is a broccoli recipe from Love and Lemons, a well-known recipe site created by Jeanine Donofrio. It is simple, versatile, and nutritious -- a perfect side dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces Broccoli Florets
  • EVOO
  • Sea Salt
  • Freshly ground Black Pepper
  • Meyer Lemons, halved, optional
  • Red Pepper flakes, optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place the florets and lemon halves onto the baking sheet. Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper, and evenly spread on the baking sheet.
  3. Roast 15 to 22 minutes or until browned around the edges.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice onto broccoli and sprinkle with red pepper flakes, if desired.
