Cornbread Recipe

Description

This is a cornbread recipe from Love and Lemons, a well-known recipe site created by Jeanine Donofrio. This cornbread is crumbly, golden, somewhat savory, and somewhat sweet. It is inspired by Midwestern roots, and pairs well with a hot bowl of chili on a cold evening.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cornmeal
  • 1 cup plus 1 tablespoon All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 cup Almond Milk
  • 1 large Egg
  • EVOO
  • 1/4 cup Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sea Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit, and grease 8x8 baking pan or line with parchment paper.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in large bowl.
  3. Combine wet ingredients in medium bowl.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dr ingredients and stir until just combined. Pour the batter into pan, and bake for 25 to 32 minutes or until the center springs back and forth to the touch and a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool for 20 minutes before slicing.
